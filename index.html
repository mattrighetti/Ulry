Ulry

Fast and lightweight read-it-later and link archiver application for iOS

Minimalistic UI

Ulry will only show you what you need, all your groups and tags for a faster than ever search

All in a single place

Find all your saved links in a single place, you can order them by different parameters and see which ones you starred or are unread

Need more details?

Ulry will show you everything for each link that you specify, you can even add notes to it!

Highly customisable

You can customize your categories with the entire color spectrum and a lot of different icons

Redirections

Are you tired of paywalled articles or website that require you to sign in? Ulry got you covered! With redirections you can tell Ulry which websites you want to be redirected to.
